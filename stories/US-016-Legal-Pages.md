# US-016: Legal Pages

## Description
As a site owner, I want to display legal information (Impressum, Privacy Policy, Terms of Service) so that the application complies with legal requirements (e.g., GDPR).

## Acceptance Criteria
1.  **Pages**: Dedicated pages for:
    -   Impressum (Legal Notice)
    -   Privacy Policy
    -   Terms of Service
2.  **Navigation**: Links to these pages in the footer of the application.
3.  **Content**: Placeholder or provided legal text.

## Technical Notes
-   Create new route `/legal/*` or individual routes.
-   Create `Footer` component.
