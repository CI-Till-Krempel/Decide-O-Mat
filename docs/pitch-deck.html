The Problem

Group decisions are messy & slow

  • 💬

    Endless chat threads. Pros and cons buried in group chats, emails, or Slack messages nobody can find later.

  • 🔒

    Privacy concerns. Sensitive decisions shared through platforms that scan content or require you to hand over your data.

  • 🚧

    Friction kills participation. Heavyweight tools require sign-ups, installs, and onboarding — most people don't bother.

Core Features

Everything a decision needs

Create & Share

05 / 10
How It Works

Three steps, one decision

1

Create your decision

Enter a question on the home page. The decision is encrypted with a key stored only in the URL hash — invisible to the server.

2

Share the link

Send the URL to anyone. They open it, enter a display name, and start adding pros and cons — no account required.

3

Vote & decide

Arguments are voted on in real time. Cast the final Yes / No vote, close the decision, and export the result.

06 / 10
Security & Privacy

Private by design

The encryption key lives in the URL fragment (#…). It never reaches the server — only people with the link can read the content.

🔐

AES-256 E2EE

Questions, arguments, and votes are encrypted client-side using the Web Crypto API.

🌐

Capability URLs

The URL itself is the access credential. Share it to grant access; revoke access by not sharing it.

🛡️

GDPR Compliant

Full account deletion, no third-party trackers, data stored in EU (europe-west4).

🪪

Identity without friction

Firebase Auth with Google or Email. Magic links let signed-in users reclaim decisions across devices.
