# Announcing Decide-O-Mat v1.6.1: Legal Pages, Richer Sharing & Security Hardening

We are releasing **Decide-O-Mat v1.6.1** — a patch release that adds GDPR-compliant legal documents, improves the sharing experience, and closes several security vulnerabilities.

## What's New?

### Legal Documents
Decide-O-Mat now ships with a full set of legal pages required for GDPR compliance:
- **Imprint** – legal notice with publisher details.
- **Terms of Service** – clear rules for using the platform.
- **Privacy Policy** – transparent data handling information in English and German.

All documents are accessible from the footer introduced in v1.6.

### Richer Decision Sharing
When you copy a decision link, the clipboard now includes **rich text** with the decision title and creator name alongside the URL. Paste it into Slack, email, or any rich-text editor and the context travels with the link.

### Dynamic Page Title
The browser tab now shows the current app version, making it easier to confirm which build you are running during testing or support.

## What's Fixed?

- **XSS vulnerability**: Clipboard HTML is now sanitized before writing to the clipboard.
- **MagicHandler test reliability**: Resolved a flaky test timeout in the anonymous-identity magic-link flow.
- **Dependency security**: Resolved high- and medium-severity vulnerabilities across frontend and backend dependencies (lodash, path-to-regexp, node-forge, undici, firebase-functions, and others).

## What's Next?
We are working on **v1.7**, which will bring **Private Decisions** with invite-only participation and enhanced notification capabilities.
