# Announcing Decide-O-Mat v1.6.2: Privacy Policy Update & Security Fix

We are releasing **Decide-O-Mat v1.6.2** — a focused patch that completes the GDPR legal documents and hardens our internal tooling against a reported security vulnerability.

## What's New?

### Data Protection Offices in Privacy Policy
The Privacy Policy now lists the responsible data protection supervisory authorities for both the German and English versions, completing the GDPR-required contact information for users who wish to lodge a complaint with a regulatory body.

## What's Fixed?

- **Prompt Injection in Issue Triage Workflow**: The internal Gemini-powered issue triage GitHub Action was vulnerable to agentic workflow injection (reported in #238). Attacker-controlled issue content was interpolated directly into the model prompt, giving it the same trust level as operator instructions. The fix wraps all user-submitted content in explicit `<issue_content>` XML tags with a surrounding security notice and post-content reminder, establishing a clear trust boundary. A failure condition for out-of-allowlist labels was also added to close the specific gap demonstrated in the proof-of-concept.

## What's Next?
We are working on **v1.7**, which will bring **Private Decisions** with invite-only participation and enhanced notification capabilities.
